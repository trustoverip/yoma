artificial intelligence #yoma

A non-human actor which executes machine-readable rules or performs transactions in order to achieve objectives of a party for which it is an agent.

donor #yoma

A Yoma role fufilled by any Yoma member who 1) has the objective of providing resources (e.g. money) to the Yoma community, 2) does not directly transact on the Yoma platform, and 3) may not receive any direct financial reward in exchange for their donation.

version 3, commit 41bab19, created 2021-10-30, last modified 2021-12-04, contributors Nicky Hickman

I
impact task #yoma

A type of opportunity that is listed on the Yoma platform and which has positive environmental or social benefits to the communities in which Yoma operates.

version 1, commit ffb4ef5, created 2021-12-04, contributors Nicky Hickman

M
marketplace #yoma

An organization that makes use of the Yoma platform so that Yoma members can exchange tokens for goods and services provided by marketplace suppliers.

version 1, commit 6f285c5, created 2021-12-04, contributors Nicky Hickman

marketplace supplier #yoma

A party whose objective is to sell goods or services in exchange for tokens that can be earned by Yoma members through their transactions on the Yoma platform

version 1, commit 072d740, created 2021-12-04, contributors Nicky Hickman

T
token provider #yoma

A yoma role which any Yoma member can perform whose objective is to promote the growth of the Yoma ecosystem by providing tokens which can be earned or exchanged on the Yoma platform.

version 1, commit de585b6, created 2021-12-04, contributors Nicky Hickman

Y
yoma community #yoma

An organization that (1) pursues the realization of the Yoma objectives, (2) has established a set of (extension) Yoma Rules to which every one of its members has committed, and (3) one such member has additionally committed to fulfill the role of administering authority.

version 1, commit aa2707e, created 2021-12-04, contributors Nicky Hickman

Yoma ecosystem governance authority #yoma

The Organisation that maintains, administers and enforces the Ecosystem Governance Framework

version 1, commit befdb35, created 2021-07-05, contributors Daniel Hardman

yoma member #yoma

A party that has committed itself to the Yoma Rules in a legally enforceable manner, for at least one specific yoma role. Hence, a yoma member (is expected to) contribute to the the realization of the yoma objectives.
